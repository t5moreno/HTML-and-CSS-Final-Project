Traverse City




Traverse City has so much to offer. There is something for everyone in the family. One of the best things to do is to go to the Sleeping Bear Dunes, running up and down the dunes, playing in Lake Michigan. This place has been voted the most beautiful place in America. Another thing Traverse City has to offer is its many festivals, from beer to cherries and everything in between. One festival that comes to mind is the National Cherry Festival. This festival happens at the end of June and runs throught the first week of July. Every year there is an airshow, Which brings in the Navy Blue Angels, which is a fan favorites. Traverse City also has lakes, streams, hiking trails, snowmoblie trails, and so much more. If you are looking to get away for a weekend or week, why not visit Traverse City. This may become your favorite place.
